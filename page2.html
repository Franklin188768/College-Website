Why?

You might feel that college isn't really important or that it's not the right fit for you, or maybe you haven't considered it much at all. Regardless, I think it's worth giving it a chance.

  • Community colleges are free in MA.
  • College can be a gateway to tons of great memories.
  • The higher your education, the more opportunities arise.

The only way to know if you're fully prepared is by starting a planner.
